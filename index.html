About Me

Hey there! I'm Pola, a 33-year-old living my best life in Texas. By day, I'm navigating the streets as an Uber driver, meeting interesting people and hearing their stories. But when I'm off the clock, my world transforms into a digital playground.

Gaming isn't just a hobby for me - it's a passion.
